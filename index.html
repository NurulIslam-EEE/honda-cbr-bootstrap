Changing your Honda’s oil at the right time will help to keep your vehicle running smoothly. To help get the most out of your Honda, it has been equipped with a Maintenance Minder system. The Maintenance Minder tracks the intervals between oil changes based on your driving habits. Once the “oil life” indicator reaches 15%, you’ll be notified each time you start the vehicle that you should consider changing the vehicle’s oil. It’s recommended that you have the oil changed by your local Honda dealership before the indicator reaches 0% to ensure the best performance.

American Honda recommends using unleaded gasoline with a minimum pump octane of 87 or higher for most gasoline-powered Honda vehicles. For a few vehicles, American Honda recommends using unleaded premium gasoline, such as pump octane 91 for best performance. Use of gasoline with a minimum pump octane below 87 can lead to engine damage. The Clarity Fuel Cell requires compressed hydrogen gas. To find the nearest hydrogen fueling station, refer to California Fuel Cell Partnership. The Civic GX (2015) requires compressed natural gas. To find the nearest natural gas fueling station, refer to the U.S. Department of Energy.
